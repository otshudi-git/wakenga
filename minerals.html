Premium Minerals from the Heart of Africa

Discover the high-quality minerals that Wakenga Mining extracts, processes, and delivers to global markets

The Democratic Republic of Congo is blessed with abundant mineral resources that play a crucial role in global industries and technologies. At Wakenga Mining SARL, we specialize in the responsible extraction and processing of these valuable minerals, ensuring that they meet international quality standards while being sourced ethically and sustainably.

Our comprehensive approach to mineral extraction involves state-of-the-art technologies, expert geological knowledge, and a deep commitment to environmental stewardship and community development.

Gold

Gold remains one of the world's most precious metals, valued for its beauty, rarity, and unique physical properties. At Wakenga Mining, we extract high-quality gold from our mines in Maniema Province, using responsible mining practices that minimize environmental impact while maximizing resource efficiency.

Properties and Applications

Gold is prized for its exceptional malleability, conductivity, and resistance to corrosion. These properties make it invaluable for a wide range of applications:

  • Jewelry and decorative arts
  • Electronics and telecommunications
  • Medical devices and dental work
  • Aerospace technologies
  • Financial reserves and investment

Our Gold Operations

Our gold mining operations employ a combination of traditional and modern extraction techniques, ensuring both efficiency and sustainability. All our gold mining and processing activities adhere to international standards and best practices.

Purity 99.5%+
Annual Production 500kg
Mining Method Open-pit & Underground
Certifications LBMA, RMI

Copper

Copper is one of the most versatile and essential metals in modern infrastructure and technology. Wakenga Mining operates significant copper extraction facilities in the Lualaba Province, where we produce high-grade copper for industrial applications worldwide.

Properties and Applications

Copper's exceptional electrical conductivity, thermal properties, and corrosion resistance make it indispensable for:

  • Electrical wiring and components
  • Plumbing and infrastructure
  • Renewable energy systems
  • Transportation equipment
  • Telecommunications
  • Industrial machinery

Our Copper Operations

Our copper mining operations include both open-pit and underground mines, followed by concentration and refining processes to produce high-purity copper products. We employ advanced techniques to maximize recovery while minimizing environmental footprint.

Purity 99.99%
Annual Production 50,000 tons
Mining Method Open-pit & Underground
Certifications ISO 9001, RMI

Cobalt

Cobalt has emerged as one of the most critical minerals of the 21st century, playing a vital role in rechargeable batteries that power our modern world. The Democratic Republic of Congo holds approximately 70% of global cobalt reserves, and Wakenga Mining is proud to be a responsible producer of this essential mineral.

Properties and Applications

Cobalt's unique properties make it invaluable for numerous high-tech applications:

  • Lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles and electronics
  • Superalloys for aerospace and gas turbines
  • Catalysts for chemical processes
  • Magnetic materials
  • Healthcare and medical applications

Our Cobalt Operations

We extract cobalt primarily as a by-product of copper mining in our Kolwezi operations. Our integrated approach ensures efficient recovery of this valuable mineral while maintaining strict adherence to responsible sourcing principles.

Purity 99.8%
Annual Production 8,000 tons
Mining Method Co-product with Copper
Certifications RMI, IRMA

Coltan

Coltan (columbite-tantalite) is a critical mineral for modern electronics and advanced technologies. Wakenga Mining extracts and processes coltan from our North Kivu operations, ensuring conflict-free sourcing and responsible mining practices.

Properties and Applications

Coltan is processed to extract tantalum, which has unique properties including extreme heat resistance and high capacitance. Key applications include:

  • Electronic capacitors for smartphones, computers, and gaming consoles
  • Aerospace components and superalloys
  • Medical implants and surgical instruments
  • Advanced optics and camera lenses
  • Nuclear reactors and specialized equipment

Our Coltan Operations

We operate our coltan mines with a strong emphasis on conflict-free sourcing, transparency, and traceability. Our operations are regularly audited to ensure compliance with international standards and responsible mining practices.

Purity 98%+
Annual Production 300 tons
Mining Method Artisanal & Small-scale
Certifications ITSCI, RMI
