Our Story

Wakenga Mining SARL was founded in 2015 with a vision to become a leading player in the mineral extraction industry in the Democratic Republic of Congo. Starting with a single mining operation focused on copper, we have grown to encompass multiple sites across the country, extracting and processing a variety of high-value minerals including gold, cobalt, and coltan.

Throughout our journey, we have maintained a steadfast commitment to responsible mining practices, community development, and environmental stewardship. Today, we stand as a testament to what can be achieved when business excellence meets social responsibility.

Our Mission

To extract and process high-quality minerals through innovative and responsible mining practices that benefit our stakeholders, communities, and the environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading mining company in Central Africa, recognized for our operational excellence, environmental stewardship, and positive social impact.

Our Values

  • Integrity in all our operations
  • Excellence in mineral extraction and processing
  • Responsibility toward communities and the environment
  • Innovation in mining techniques and processes
  • Safety as our top priority
