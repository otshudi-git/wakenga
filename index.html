About Wakenga Mining

Established in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Wakenga Mining SARL is a leading player in the mining industry, specializing in the exploration, extraction, processing, and export of high-quality minerals.

With a commitment to sustainable practices and community development, we leverage cutting-edge technology and expert knowledge to deliver premium mineral products to global markets.
